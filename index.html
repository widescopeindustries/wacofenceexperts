Frequently Asked Questions

How much does fence installation cost?

Fence costs vary based on materials, height, length, and terrain. Cedar privacy fences typically range $20-35 per linear foot installed. Chain link runs $10-20 per foot, and vinyl $25-40 per foot. We provide free, detailed estimates with no obligation.

How long does installation take?

Most residential fence installations take 2-5 days depending on size and complexity. We'll provide a specific timeline during your free estimate. Weather and ground conditions can affect the schedule.

Do I need a permit for a fence in Waco?

In most cases, yes. The City of Waco requires permits for fences over 6 feet tall. We handle all permitting and ensure your fence meets local codes and HOA requirements.

What's the best fence material for Texas weather?

Cedar is naturally rot-resistant and handles Texas heat well. Vinyl never needs painting and resists warping. Chain link is extremely durable. We'll recommend the best option based on your budget, needs, and property.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes! We provide a 1-year workmanship warranty on all installations. Material warranties vary by manufacturer - cedar and vinyl typically come with 10+ year warranties.

Can you repair my existing fence?

Absolutely! We repair damaged posts, replace broken pickets, fix gates, and restain weathered wood. Often repairs are more cost-effective than full replacement. Call for same-day assessment.
