Commercial Fence Types We Install

Chain Link Fencing

  • Commercial Grade: Heavy-gauge galvanized chain link (9-gauge or 6-gauge)
  • Height Options: 4' to 12' tall for various security levels
  • Add-Ons: Barbed wire, razor wire, privacy slats, windscreen
  • Gates: Single swing, double drive, cantilever sliding gates
  • Best For: Perimeter security, warehouses, industrial facilities

Ornamental Aluminum & Steel

  • Styles: Classic picket, arched, spear-top designs
  • Durability: Powder-coated finish resists rust and fading
  • Professional Look: Elegant appearance for upscale properties
  • Best For: Office parks, medical facilities, upscale apartments

Vinyl Commercial Fencing

  • Maintenance-Free: Never needs painting or staining
  • Styles: Privacy, semi-privacy, picket designs
  • Durability: Impact-resistant, won't rot or rust
  • Best For: Apartment complexes, HOAs, schools

Wood Privacy Fencing

  • Materials: Cedar or treated pine
  • Privacy: Complete visual barrier between properties
  • Customizable: Various heights, styles, and stain colors
  • Best For: Residential areas in multi-family properties

Commercial Fencing FAQs

Do commercial properties require permits for fencing in Waco?

Yes, most commercial fencing projects in Waco require building permits. The requirements vary based on fence height, location, and property zoning. We handle all permit applications and ensure your fence meets all local codes and setback requirements.

How long does commercial fence installation take?

Timeline depends on project scope. Small projects (50-100 linear feet) typically take 2-3 days. Larger perimeter fencing for warehouses or apartment complexes may take 1-2 weeks. We provide detailed timelines with every proposal and work efficiently to minimize disruption to your operations.

What fence height is required for commercial pools in Texas?

Texas requires pool fencing to be at least 48" (4 feet) tall with no gaps larger than 4 inches. Gates must be self-closing and self-latching with latches at least 54" from ground level. We ensure all pool fencing meets state and local safety codes.

Can you install fencing while our business is operating?

Yes! We work with your schedule to minimize disruption. For many businesses, we can install fencing during off-hours or weekends. We'll coordinate with you to develop an installation schedule that works for your operations.

Do you offer maintenance plans for commercial fencing?

Yes, we provide maintenance and repair services for all fence types. This includes annual inspections, gate adjustments, operator service, and damage repairs. Contact us to discuss a maintenance plan tailored to your property's needs.

What's the most cost-effective commercial fencing option?

Chain link fencing offers the best value for large commercial properties. It's durable, low-maintenance, and costs $8-15 per linear foot for standard installations. For properties requiring a more upscale appearance, vinyl or ornamental aluminum provides excellent longevity with minimal maintenance.
