Looking for a beautiful, long-lasting cedar fence for your Waco property? Waco Fence Experts specializes in premium cedar fence installation throughout McLennan County. Whether you need a privacy fence for your backyard, a decorative fence for your front yard, or commercial cedar fencing, we deliver exceptional craftsmanship using the highest quality materials.

Cedar is the gold standard for wood fencing in Texas. Its natural resistance to rot, insects, and decay makes it the perfect choice for our hot, humid climate. Unlike treated pine, cedar won't warp or crack as easily, and it ages beautifully with minimal maintenance.

Why Choose Cedar Fencing for Your Waco Property?

Cedar fencing offers unmatched benefits for Texas homeowners and businesses:

  • Natural Durability: Cedar contains natural oils that repel insects and resist rot, lasting 20-25+ years
  • Weather Resistance: Stands up to Waco's intense sun, humidity, and occasional storms
  • Beautiful Appearance: Rich, warm tones that enhance your property's curb appeal
  • Low Maintenance: No painting required - optional staining every few years
  • Privacy & Security: Solid panels provide complete privacy and security
  • Property Value: Increases your home's resale value significantly
  • Eco-Friendly: Sustainable, renewable resource that's biodegradable

Types of Cedar We Install

Western Red Cedar

The premium choice for Waco homeowners. Western red cedar is the top-of-the-line option, known for its exceptional longevity of 25+ years. This dense wood naturally resists moisture, preventing warping and cracking even in Texas heat. Its rich reddish-brown color and straight grain pattern make it the most beautiful option available.

Best for: Homeowners wanting the longest-lasting, most beautiful fence possible

Imported Cedar (Japanese Cedar)

A cost-effective alternative that still delivers excellent quality. Imported cedar offers 20-year longevity at a more affordable price point. While slightly less dense than western red cedar, it's still far superior to treated pine and provides excellent value for budget-conscious homeowners.

Best for: New construction, rental properties, or homeowners seeking quality on a budget

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a cedar fence last in Waco, TX?

Western red cedar lasts 25+ years, while imported cedar lasts 20+ years with minimal maintenance. This is significantly longer than treated pine (10-15 years). The key factors are cedar type, proper installation, and optional staining every 3-5 years.

Do I need to stain my cedar fence?

No, staining is optional. Cedar naturally weathers to a beautiful silver-gray patina. However, many homeowners choose to stain their fence to maintain the rich cedar color and add extra UV protection. We recommend an oil-based stain if you choose to stain.

What's the difference between western red cedar and imported cedar?

Western red cedar is denser, more resistant to warping, and lasts 25+ years. It's reddish-brown and the premium option. Imported (Japanese) cedar is less dense, slightly more prone to warping, but still excellent quality and more affordable. It lasts 20+ years. Both are far superior to treated pine.

Can cedar fencing withstand Texas weather?

Absolutely! Cedar is one of the best woods for Texas climate. Its natural oils resist moisture and humidity, it doesn't crack in our intense heat, and it holds up well against storms. Cedar is specifically chosen for Texas because it thrives in our conditions.

Do I need a permit for a fence in Waco?

Residential fences in Waco typically don't require a permit if they're 6 feet or shorter and meet setback requirements. However, corner lots have special visibility requirements. We handle all the details and ensure your fence complies with Waco city codes.

How tall can my cedar fence be in Waco?

Residential fences in Waco can be up to 6 feet tall in most cases. Taller fences may be allowed depending on your property and location. We'll verify regulations for your specific property during our consultation.
